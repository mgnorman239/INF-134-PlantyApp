Meet the Development Team

As a team, we were looking for a way to help those suffering with mental health find a solution. However; we discovered that there are many problems that fall under the mental health umbrella, each with a different solution.

This led us to the development of Planty App. Instead of attempting to solve all mental health problems, we focused on promoting a solution that many mental health problems can benefit from.
